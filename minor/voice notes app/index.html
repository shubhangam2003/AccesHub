How to Use

Get started with Voice Notes App by following these simple instructions:

1. Start Listening

Click the "Start Listening" button to activate voice recognition.

2. Speak Commands

Use voice commands to create notes, delete notes, or read notes aloud.

3. Save Your Note

Click the "Save Note" button to store your voice-created note.

4. View and Manage

Explore your notes by clicking the "View Saved Notes" button and download your notes by clicking the "Download" button.

5. Edit your Notes

Edit your notes by clicking the "Edit Notes" button.

Note: Ensure your microphone is enabled and properly configured for optimal performance.
